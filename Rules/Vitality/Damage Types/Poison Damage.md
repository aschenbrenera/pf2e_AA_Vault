# Poison Damage

Venoms, toxins and the like can deal [[Rules/Traits/Effect/Poison]] damage, which affects creatures by way of contact, ingestion, inhalation, or injury. In addition to coming from monster attacks, alchemical items, and spells, poison damage is often caused by ongoing [[Afflictions]].
