# Paranoia

**Description:**  The constant anxiety of the situation has begun to wear on you.

## Mechanical Rules

- This condition acts exactly as Frightened, but it does not dissipate as normal. The player must re-roll their save and succeed to lose this condition. Your Paranoia value starts at 1 and can increase and decrease.
	- The player get's a natural opportunity to re-roll their save every 10 minutes from the time of receiving the condition.
- Inherit Frightened
	- You take a status penalty equal to your frightened value to all your checks and DCs (including AC).
