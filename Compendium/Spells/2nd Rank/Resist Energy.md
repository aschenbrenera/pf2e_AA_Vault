# Resist Energy  &emsp;*(Spell 2)*

[Concentrate](Concentrate.md "Action & Ability Trait") [Manipulate](Manipulate.md "General Trait") 
**Description:** A shield of elemental energy protects a creature against one type of energy damage.

**Traditions:** arcane, divine, occult, primal 
**Bloodline:** elemental
**Catalysts:** [Chaos Falcon Feather](https://2e.aonprd.com/Equipment.aspx?ID=3257)

**Range:** Touch
**Targets:** 1 creature
**Duration:** 10 minutes
**Activity:** Two Action ⬥⬥

## Mechanical Rules

- Choose acid, cold, electricity, fire, or sonic damage. The target and its gear gain resistance 5 against the damage type you chose.

> [!info] Heighten
>- **Heightened (4th):** The resistance increases to 10, and you can target up to two creatures.
>- **Heightened (7th):** The resistance increases to 15, and you can target up to five creatures.
