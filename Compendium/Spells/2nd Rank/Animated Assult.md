# Animated Assult [](#Actions "Two-Action") &emsp;*(Spell 1)*

[Concentrate](Concentrate.md "Action & Ability Trait") [Manipulate](Manipulate.md "General Trait")
**Description:** You use your mind to manipulate unattended objects in the area, temporarily animating them to attack. The objects hover in the air, then hurl themselves at nearby creatures in a chaotic flurry of debris.

**Traditions:** arcane, occult

**Range:** 120 feet
**Area:** 10-foot burst
**Duration:** sustained up to 1 minute
**Defense:** [[Basic Saving Throw|Basic]] Reflex
**Activity:** Two Action ⬥⬥

## Mechanical Rules

- This assault deals 2d10 bludgeoning damage (basic Reflex save) to each creature in the area.
- On subsequent rounds, the first time each round you Sustain this spell, it deals 1d10 bludgeoning damage (basic Reflex save) to each creature in the area.
  
> [!info] Heighten
>- **Heightened (+2):** The initial damage increases by 2d10, and the subsequent damage increases by 1d10.
