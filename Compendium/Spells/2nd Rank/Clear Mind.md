# Clear Mind &emsp;*(Spell 2)*

[Concentrate](Concentrate.md "Action & Ability Trait") [Manipulate](Manipulate.md "General Trait") [Healing](Healing.md "Effect Trait") [Mental](Mental.md "Effect Trait") 
**Description:** You drive mental contamination from the target's mind.

**Traditions:** divine, occult, primal

**Range:** Touch
**Targets:** 1 willing creature
**Activity:** Two Action ⬥⬥

## Mechanical Rules

- Attempt to [[Counteracting|Counteract]] an effect of your choice imposing one of these conditions on the target: [[Fleeing]], [[Frightened]], [[Stupefied]].
	- If you failed to counteract the effect but you would have if its counteract rank were 2 lower, instead suppress the effect until the beginning of your next turn.
	- The effect's duration doesn't elapse while it's suppressed. This spell can't counteract or suppress conditions that are part of curses, diseases, or a natural state of the target.

> [!info] Heighten
>- **Heightened (4th):** Add [[Confused]], [[Controlled]], and [[Slowed]] to the list of conditions.
>- **Heightened (6th):** Add [[Doomed]] to the list of conditions.
>- **Heightened (8th):** Add [[Stunned]] to the list of conditions.
