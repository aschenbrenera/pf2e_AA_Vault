# Puff of Poison [](#Actions "Two-Action") &emsp;*(Cantrip 1)*

[Cantrip](Cantrip.md "General Trait") [Concentrate](Concentrate.md "Action & Ability Trait") [Manipulate](Manipulate.md "General Trait") [Poison](Poison.md "Effect Trait") 
**Description:** You exhale a shimmering cloud of toxic breath at an enemy's face.

**Traditions:** arcane, primal

**Range:** 10 feet
**Targets:** 1 creature
**Defense:** Fortitude
**Activity:** Two Action ⬥⬥

## Mechanical Rules

- The target takes 1d4 [[Poison Damage]] and 1d4 [[Persistent Damage]], depending on its Fortitude save.  
  
  > [!success-degree]
  > - **Critical Success** The creature is unaffected.  
  > - **Success** The target takes half initial damage and no persistent damage.  
  > - **Failure** The target takes full initial and persistent damage.  
  > - **Critical Failure** The target takes double initial and persistent damage.

> [!info] Heighten
>- **Heightened (+2):** The initial poison damage increases by 1d4, and the persistent poison damage increases by 1d4.

## Gameplay Interaction

- Cantrip spells are automatically heightened to half your level rounded up.
