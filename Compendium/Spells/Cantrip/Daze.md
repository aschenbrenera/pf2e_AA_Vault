# Daze [](#Actions "Two-Action") &emsp;*(Cantrip 1)*

[Cantrip](Cantrip.md "General Trait") [Concentrate](Concentrate.md "Action & Ability Trait")  [Manipulate](Manipulate.md "General Trait") [Mental](Mental.md "Effect Trait") [Nonlethal](Nonlethal.md "Effect Trait")
**Description:** You push into the target's mind and daze it with a mental jolt. 

**Traditions:** arcane, divine, occult
**Bloodline:** aberant, hag

**Range:** 60-feet
**Targets:** 1 creature
**Defense:** Will
**Duration:** 1 round
**Activity:** Two Action ⬥⬥

## Mechanical Rules

- The jolt deals 1d6 mental damage, with a basic Will save.
	- If the target critically fails the save, it is also [[Stunned]] 1.

> [!info] Heighten
>- **Heightened (+2):** The damage increases by 1d6.

## Gameplay Interaction

- Cantrip spells are automatically heightened to half your level rounded up.
