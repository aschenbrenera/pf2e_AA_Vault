# Telekinetic Hand [](#Actions "Two-Action") &emsp;*(Cantrip 1)*

[Cantrip](Cantrip.md "General Trait") [Concentrate](Concentrate.md "Action & Ability Trait") [Manipulate](Manipulate.md "General Trait") 
**Description:** You create a floating, magical hand that moves a single object.

**Traditions:** arcane, occult

**Range:** 30 feet
**Targets:** 1 unattended object of Light bulk or less
**Duration:** sustained
**Activity:** Two Action ⬥⬥

## Mechanical Rules

- You create a floating, magical hand, that is either invisible or ghostlike, that grasps the target object.
	- It slowly moves the object up to 20 feet in any direction.
	- When you Sustain the spell, you can move the object an additional 20 feet.
	- If the object is in the air when the spell ends, the object falls.

> [!info] Heighten
>- **Heightened (3rd):** You can target an unattended object with a Bulk of 1 or less.
>- **Heightened (5th):** The range increases to 60 feet, and you can target an unattended object with a Bulk of 1 or less.
>- **Heightened (7th):** The range increases to 60 feet, and you can target an unattended object with a Bulk of 2 or less.

## Gameplay Interaction

- Cantrip spells are automatically heightened to half your level rounded up.
