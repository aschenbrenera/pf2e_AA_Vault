# Bless [](#Actions "Two-Action") &emsp;*(Spell 1)*

[Concentrate](Concentrate.md "Action & Ability Trait") [Manipulate](Manipulate.md "General Trait") [Aura](Aura.md "Effect Trait") [Mental](Mental.md "Effect Trait")
**Description:** Blessings from beyond help your companions strike true.

**Traditions:** divine, occult 

**Area:** 15-foot emanation
**Duration:** 1 minute
**Activity:** Two Action ⬥⬥

## Mechanical Rules

- You and your allies gain a +1 status bonus to attack rolls while within the emanation.
- Once per round on subsequent turns, you can Sustain the spell to increase the emanation's radius by 10 feet. 
- _Bless_ can counteract [[Bane]].
