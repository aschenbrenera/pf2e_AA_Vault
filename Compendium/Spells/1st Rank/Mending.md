# Mending  &emsp;*(Spell 1)*

[Concentrate](Concentrate.md "Action & Ability Trait") [Manipulate](Manipulate.md "General Trait") 
**Description:** You repair the target item.

**Traditions:** arcane, divine, occult, primal 

**Range:** Touch
**Targets:** Non-magical object of light Bulk or less
**Activity:** Cast 10 minutes

## Mechanical Rules

- You restore 5 Hit Points per spell rank to the target, potentially removing the broken condition if this repairs it past the item's Broken Threshold.
- You can't replace lost pieces or repair an object that's been completely destroyed.

> [!info] Heighten
>- **Heightened (2nd):** You can target a non-magical object of 1 Bulk or less.
>- **Heightened (3rd):** You can target a non-magical object of 2 Bulk or less, or a magical object of 1 Bulk or less.
