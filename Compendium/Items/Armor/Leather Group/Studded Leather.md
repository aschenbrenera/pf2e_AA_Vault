# Studded Leather

This leather armor is reinforced with metal studs and sometimes small metal plates, providing most of the flexibility of leather armor with more robust protection.

**Price:** 3 gp
**Bulk:** 2

**Category:** Light
**Group:** Leather
**AC Bonus:** +2
**Dex Cap:** +3
**Check Penalty:** -1
**Speed Penalty:** -
**Strength:** +1

## Gameplay Interaction

- Armor Specialization - [[Leather Group]] (when granted by an ability)
	- You gain resistance to bludgeoning damage equal to 1 + the value of the armor’s potency rune for medium armor, or 2 + the value of the armor’s potency rune for heavy armor.
