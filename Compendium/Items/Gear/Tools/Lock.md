# Lock

**Bulk:** -
**Hands:** 2

Picking a lock requires multiple successful [[Pick a Lock]] checks.

| **Level** | **Item**        | **Price** | **Picking Success** | 
| --------- | --------------- | --------- | ------------------- |
| 0         | Lock (Poor)     | 2 sp      | 2 successful; DC 15 |
| 1         | Lock (Simple)   | 2 gp      | 3 successful; DC 20 |
| 3         | Lock (Average)  | 15 gp     | 4 successful; DC 25 |
| 9         | Lock (Good)     | 200 gp    | 5 successful; DC 30 |
| 17        | Lock (Superior) | 4,500 gp  | 6 successful; DC 40 |

## Gameplay Interaction

- [[Force Open]]
	- The DC to Force Open something that is locked uses the Thievery DC of its lock but adjusts it to be very hard (increasing the DC by 5).

## Gameplay Tip

If you want to allow a player to try to break a lock with weapon attacks, decide if the weapon is fit for doing so, and then you can use the suggested table below.
