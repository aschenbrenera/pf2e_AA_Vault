# Darkvision Elixir

[Consumable](Consumable.md "Item Trait") [Alchemical](Alchemical.md "Item Trait") [Elixir](Elixir.md "Item Trait")
 
**Bulk:** L
**Hand Usage:** Held in 1 hand
**Activate:** [](#Actions "Single Action") (manipulate)

After you drink this elixir, your sight becomes sharper in darkness. 
- You gain [[Darkvision]] for the listed duration.

| **Level** | **Item** | **Price** | Effect                                                                                                                          |
| --------- | -------- | --------- | -------------------------- |
| 2         | Lesser   | 6 gp      | The duration is 10 minutes. |
| 4         | Moderate | 11 gp     | The duration is 1 hour.     |
| 8         | Greater  | 90 gp     | The duration is 24 hours.   |
