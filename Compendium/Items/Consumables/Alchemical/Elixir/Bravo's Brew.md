# Bravo's Brew

[Consumable](Consumable.md "Item Trait") [Alchemical](Alchemical.md "Item Trait") [Elixir](Elixir.md "Item Trait") [Mental](Mental.md "Effect Trait")
 
**Bulk:** L
**Hand Usage:** Held in 1 hand
**Activate:** [](#Actions "Single Action") (manipulate)

This flask of foaming beer grants courage. 

- For the next hour after drinking this elixir, you gain an item bonus to Will saves, which is greater when attempting Will saves against fear.

| **Level** | **Item** | **Price** | Effect                                                                                                                          |
| --------- | -------- | --------- | -------------------------- |
| 2         | Lesser   | 7 gp      | The bonus on Will saves is +1, or +2 against fear.   |
| 10        | Moderate | 150 gp    | The bonus on Will saves is +2, or +3 against fear. |
| 15        | Greater  | 700 gp    | The bonus on Will saves is +3, or +4 against fear. If you roll a success on a save against fear, you get a critical success instead.     |
