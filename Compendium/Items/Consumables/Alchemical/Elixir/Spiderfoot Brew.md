# Spiderfoot Brew

[Consumable](Consumable.md "Item Trait") [Alchemical](Alchemical.md "Item Trait") [Elixir](Elixir.md "Item Trait") [Morph](Morph.md "Effect Trait")
 
**Bulk:** L
**Hand Usage:** Held in 1 hand
**Activate:** [](#Actions "Single Action") (manipulate)

This sticky fluid is made from the silk glands of giant spiders. When you drink a spiderfoot brew, tiny hairs grow on your hands and feet.

- You gain climb Speed and an item bonus to Athletics checks made to Climb for the listed duration.

| **Level** | **Item** | **Price** | Effect                                                                                                                          |
| --------- | -------- | --------- | -------------------------- |
| 3         | Lesser   | 12 gp     | The climb Speed is 15 feet, the item bonus is +1, and the duration is 1 minute.   |
| 9         | Moderate | 150 gp    | The climb Speed is 20 feet, the item bonus is +2, and the duration is 10 minutes. |
| 17        | Greater  | 2,500 gp  | The climb Speed is 25 feet, the item bonus is +3, and the duration is 1 hour.     |
