# Bottled Lightning

[Consumable](Consumable.md "Item Trait") [Alchemical](Alchemical.md "Item Trait") [Bomb](Bomb.md "Item Trait") [Electricity](Electricity.md "Effect Trait") [Splash](Splash.md "Effect Trait")
 
**Bulk:** L
**Hand Usage:** held in 1 hand
**Activate:** [](#Actions "Single Action") Strike

Bottled lightning is packed with volatile reagents that create a blast of electricity when they're exposed to air.

- On a hit, the target becomes [[Off-Guard]] until the start of your next turn. Many types grant an item bonus to attack rolls.


| **Level** | **Item** | **Price** | Effect                     |
| --------- | -------- | --------- | -------------------------- |
| 1         | Lesser   | 3 gp      | The bomb deals 1d6 electricity damage and 1 electricity splash damage. |
| 3         | Moderate | 10 gp     | You gain a +1 item bonus to attack rolls. The bomb deals 2d6 electricity damage and 2 electricity splash damage. |
| 11         | Greater | 250 gp    | You gain a +2 item bonus to attack rolls. The bomb deals 3d6 electricity damage and 3 electricity splash damage. |
| 17        | Major    | 2,500 gp    | You gain a +3 item bonus to attack rolls. The bomb deals 4d6 electricity damage and 4 electricity splash damage. |
