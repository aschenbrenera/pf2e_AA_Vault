# Tumbling Teamwork &emsp;*(Feat 2)*

[General](General.md "Feat Trait") [Skill](Skill.md "Feat Trait") 
**Description:** Your tumbling distracts a foe enough to create an advantage for one of your allies.
**Prerequisites:** Expert in [[Acrobatics]]

## Mechanical Rules

- When you successfully [[Tumble Through]] an enemy's space, an ally who is adjacent to that enemy can Step as a reaction, but they must remain adjacent to that enemy.
