# Acrobat Dedication &emsp;*(Feat 2)*

[Archetype](Archetype.md "Feat Trait") [Dedication](Dedication.md "Feat Trait") [Acrobat](Acrobat.md "Class Trait")

**Prerequisites:** Trained in [[Acrobatics]]

## Mechanical Rules

- You become an expert in Acrobatics.
	- At 7th level, you become a master in Acrobatics.
	- At 15th level, you become legendary in Acrobatics.
- Whenever you critically succeed at an Acrobatics check to [[Tumble Through]] an enemy's space, you don't treat the enemy's space as difficult terrain.
